Navigation Tool

GPS allows users to track mileage, speed, elevation, and location at any given moment. For climbers, this is a great tool for tracking progess.
Whether it is for circumnavigating the world or climbing mountain, the compass has been used for hundred of years for navigation. Unlike eletronic navigation systems, which requires periodic calibration and battery to operate, a compass will always and accurately points north.
When all fails, topographic maps are great alternative for navigation needs. An entire trip can be planned out with help of a topographic map, and can greatly decrease the chance of getting loss. It can also tell a lot about an area's elevation.

First-aid supplies

Having an first aid kit can mean the difference between life and death in exteme condition. Always pack accordingly because accidents do happen!
What you need:

  1. Medical tape/Duct tape
  2. Tweezers, small scissors, and safety pins
  3. Disinfecting ointment/Neosporin
  4. Adhesive bandages
